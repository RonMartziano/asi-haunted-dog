Grounded in the real Asi
🐶

The concept keeps Asi's actual signature traits from your photos: the cream-white fluffy coat, feathered pointed ears, permanent happy tongue, and that plumed tail. The unicorn-flower-crown photo even inspired the demon-mode horns — a playful nod that turns sinister. Custom hand-drawn or AI-assisted art later should preserve these so players recognize "that's Asi."

These vector portraits are the in-game placeholder sprites used by the playable prototype (same SVGs power the battle scene). They're a stylization target, not final art — next step is a proper character sheet (idle / attack / transform / hit / victory frames) for both forms.
